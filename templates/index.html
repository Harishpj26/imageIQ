What is Image Classification ?

Image Classification is a type of computer vision task that involves categorizing an image into predefined classes or labels based on its content. The goal is for an AI model to automatically identify the objects, scenes, or features present in an image, and assign it to one of the defined categories. Image classification enables machines to understand and interpret visual data in ways similar to human perception.
